One, Two, Heart, You!

Good tunes and good times in Downtown Peekskill!

12heartU is a non-profit, community focused music celebration.

  • Date: October 9-10, 2021
  • Location: 36 North Division Street, Peekskill NY
  • Cost: Free to all!

North Division Street, which is the center of Peekskill, will be closed to traffic and open to Good Times. A stage will be at one end and will be graced by our favorite independent bands and artists. This event is something the organizers, Brian and Joe, have been planning and plotting for years. This is year one. More years to come.
